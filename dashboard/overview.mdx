## Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your central command center in Reach. It provides a comprehensive overview of your outreach operations, performance metrics, and key insights at a glance.

## Key Sections

### Overview Cards

The top section displays eight key metric cards:

#### Lead Metrics
- **Total Leads**: Total number of leads in your system
- **Active Leads**: Leads currently engaged in campaigns
- **New Leads**: Leads added in the selected time period
- **Blocked Leads**: Leads who have blocked your account

#### Message Metrics
- **Messages Sent**: Total messages sent across all campaigns
- **Messages Delivered**: Successfully delivered messages
- **Response Rate**: Percentage of leads who responded
- **Open Rate**: Percentage of messages opened (if tracking enabled)

#### Deal Metrics
- **Total Deals**: All deals in your pipeline
- **Active Deals**: Deals currently in progress
- **Won Deals**: Successfully closed deals
- **Deal Value**: Total value of all deals

## Organization View

If you're an admin or owner of an organization, the dashboard can show:

- **My Data**: Only your personal metrics and campaigns
- **All Organization Data**: Aggregated metrics from all organization members

Use the view mode selector (if available) to switch between personal and organization-wide views.

## Refreshing Data

Click the **Refresh** button to update all dashboard metrics with the latest data. The dashboard automatically refreshes when you switch organizations or when data changes.

<Info>
  Dashboard data is cached for performance. Use the refresh button to get real-time updates.
</Info>

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can quickly:

- **Create New Campaign**: Click the "New Campaign" button
- **View Campaigns**: Navigate to the Campaigns page
- **View Leads**: Navigate to the Leads page
- **View Deals**: Navigate to the Deals page

## Understanding Metrics

### Lead Stats

- **Total Leads**: All leads imported or created in your account
- **Active Leads**: Leads currently included in active campaigns
- **New Leads**: Leads added within the selected time period
- **Blocked Leads**: Leads who have blocked your Telegram account

### Message Stats

- **Messages Sent**: Total count of messages sent through campaigns
- **Messages Delivered**: Messages successfully delivered to recipients
- **Response Rate**: Percentage of messages that received a response
- **Open Rate**: Percentage of messages opened (requires read receipts)

### Deal Stats

- **Total Deals**: All deals in your pipeline (active, won, and lost)
- **Active Deals**: Deals currently in progress (not won or lost)
- **Won Deals**: Successfully closed deals
- **Deal Value**: Total monetary value of all deals

## Time Periods

Dashboard metrics can be filtered by time period:

- **Today**: Metrics for the current day
- **Last 7 Days**: Metrics for the past week
- **Last 30 Days**: Metrics for the past month
- **All Time**: All available metrics

<Note>
  Time period filters may vary depending on your subscription plan.
</Note>

## Tips for Using the Dashboard

1. **Regular Monitoring**: Check your dashboard regularly to track performance trends
2. **Compare Periods**: Use time period filters to compare performance over time
3. **Identify Trends**: Look for patterns in message performance and response rates
4. **Focus on Metrics**: Pay attention to metrics that matter most to your goals
5. **Take Action**: Use insights from the dashboard to optimize your campaigns
